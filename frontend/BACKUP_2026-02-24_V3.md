## WHAT CHANGED IN V3

The entire 32-widget system was rewritten from a political intelligence framing to a **community-building intelligence** framing. The client wants to build the biggest Russian-speaking community in Armenia. The platform analyzes Telegram conversations to understand:

- **What people talk about** (housing, schools, banking, healthcare, food, coworking)
- **Their problems** (rent prices, bureaucracy, finding Russian-speaking doctors)
- **Their interests** (hiking, food, tech, kids activities)
- **Their intent** (settling in, starting a business, leaving, buying property)
- **Their behavior** (engagement patterns, integration progress, churn signals)

## KEY DESIGN DECISIONS (V3)

1. **Light theme**: bg-gray-50 (#f9fafb), white cards, gray-200 borders
2. **No political framing**: Zero references to geopolitics, polarization, or political stance
3. **Community building focus**: Every widget answers "how do we serve this community better?"
4. **Actionable insights**: Each widget includes a colored insight box with specific recommendations
5. **Real context**: Mock data uses actual Russian-community-in-Armenia topics (Yerevan neighborhoods, Armenian language learning, residency permits, etc.)
6. **Interactive elements**: Persona gallery with clickable bars, activity timeline with hourly/daily toggle, expandable AI brief
7. **Color-coded tiers**: Each of 8 sections has a distinct color (teal, blue, rose, indigo, violet, purple, emerald, slate)
8. **All widgets use Recharts** for charts (Line, Area, Radar, Bar) and custom HTML/CSS for matrices, funnels, timelines
