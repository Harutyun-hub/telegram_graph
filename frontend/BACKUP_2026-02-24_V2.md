# BACKUP V2 — Community Intelligence Platform
# Date: 2026-02-24
# Status: All 32 widgets rebuilt with community-building lens

## WHAT CHANGED FROM V1 TO V2

The entire widget system was reframed from "political intelligence / geopolitical analysis" 
to "community-building intelligence" for the Russian-speaking community in Armenia.

### Old (V1) Framing — WRONG:
- Geopolitical stance analysis (pro-Russian vs pro-Western)
- Polarization / fracture maps
- Military / border security
- Alarm wire for threats
- Political sentiment analysis

### New (V2) Framing — CORRECT:
- What people talk about (housing, education, banking, healthcare)
- Their problems and pain points (rent prices, bureaucracy, childcare)
- Their interests (hiking, food, tech meetups, language learning)
- Behavioral personas (IT Relocant, Young Family, Digital Nomad, Entrepreneur)
- Community growth, retention, and engagement funnels
- Business opportunities (unmet service needs)
- Content strategy (what content format works for what topic)
